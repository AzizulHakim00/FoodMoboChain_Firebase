# FoodMoboChain v1.3 feature map

## Marketplace data

- 12 repeatable starter stores
- 72 repeatable starter food records
- 22 categories
- 8 promotional campaigns
- 10 delivery zones
- 12 rental carts
- 12 training resources
- 20 community announcements

## Buyer

- Store directory and store-specific menus
- Food search, tags, categories and advanced sorting
- Authoritative sale prices and regular-price comparison
- Stock-aware cart
- Favourites
- Saved delivery addresses
- Store-split cash-on-delivery checkout
- Delivery notes
- Six-stage order tracking
- Reviews, notifications, rentals, training and support tickets

## Vendor

- Approval-gated storefront creation
- Store directory presence
- Image-rich menu inventory
- Official price, stock, preparation time, delivery fee and availability controls
- Customer-order progression through packed and delivered
- Seller analytics

## Administrator

- Vendor approval and content moderation
- Repeatable large-marketplace creation
- Support-ticket replies and status management
- Users, stores, foods, orders, rental and support analytics
- Firebase Security Rules deployment through GitHub Actions

## Security

- Verified-email Authentication
- App Check integration
- Store and food ownership validation
- Official price and stock validation
- Owner-only saved addresses
- User-created and administrator-managed support tickets
- Restricted order transitions
- Delivered-order-only reviews
- Atomic rental reservation dates
